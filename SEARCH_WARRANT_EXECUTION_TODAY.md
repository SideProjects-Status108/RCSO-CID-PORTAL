# Real Search Warrant Execution — Today (April 16, 2025)
## Problems Identified & Software Intervention Points

**Operation:** Narcotic search warrant  
**Target:** Motorola Moto G Power 2022 phone  
**Team Size:** 8 people  
**Result:** Warrant executed, phone recovered

---

## T+0 (10:12am) — TEXT COORDINATION

**What Happened:**
- Supervisor sends text to 8 people
- Location: Smyrna Fire Station 5 (via Apple Maps pin)
- Timing: "TBD, will advise shortly"
- Equipment: "Make sure you have a vest and radio"
- Target phone image shared
- Briefing at fire hall in 30 mins

**Problems:**
1. Location via text pin (not integrated)
2. No formal operation document
3. Equipment list scattered in text
4. Time TBD (no firm start time)
5. No checklist to verify equipment
6. **No complaint number shared**
7. No briefing document provided before arrival

---

## T+0:30 (10:42am) — BRIEFING AT FIRE HALL

**Team Assembled:**
- Supervisor
- Lead Detective (executing warrant)
- 2 Arabic/RCSO uniformed translators
- 3 Searchers
- 1 Crime Scene Tech (photography)
- 1 Detective (Oscar device — 360 photo/video)
- 1 Crime Scene Sketch Tech

**Roles Assigned Verbally** — Nothing documented

---

## CRITICAL PROBLEMS IDENTIFIED

### Problem 1: No Equipment Verification
- No one checked if all searchers had flashlights, gloves, radios
- **No charger for target phone brought** (phone was "almost dead")
- Unknown what charger type needed (USB-C? Micro USB?)
- Charger should have been brought but wasn't

### Problem 2: No Target Phone Specs Shared
- Team doesn't know phone model details
- No charger specs provided
- Can't do preliminary phone exam without charger
- Phone goes to evidence, waits for lab (delays investigation)

### Problem 3: No Complaint Number Known
- **Nobody on team knows what complaint number is**
- Will be needed for supplements
- Creates delays when filing documentation

### Problem 4: No Supplement Briefing
- **Nobody told what supplements are required after operation**
- Sketch tech doesn't know: "Do I file a supplement?"
- Oscar operator doesn't know: "Do I file a supplement?"
- Crime Scene Tech doesn't know: "Do I file a supplement?"
- Nobody gets reminded. Supplements filed late or never.

### Problem 5: No Role Cards/Assignments Documented
- Roles only exist in people's heads
- If someone injured, who takes over? Unknown
- No chain of command backup

### Problem 6: Vehicle Staging Not Pre-Planned
- Cars line up wrong initially
- **Team has to scramble around neighborhood to reset**
- Wrong approach order
- Risk of being spotted
- Wasted time before execution

### Problem 7: No Live Mapping Coordination
- Team can't see staging positions
- No real-time vehicle coordination
- No predetermined approach route

### Problem 8: Crime Scene Sketch — Double Work
- Sketch tech draws rough sketch on **paper** during operation
- Later: Must **redraw official copy** from rough sketch
- Wasted time, transcription error risk
- Should have digital sketching tool

### Problem 9: Evidence Documentation Manual
- Evidence found, photographed, logged manually
- No formal evidence intake form ready
- Chain of custody documented in notes (not structured)
- Oscar device footage: Unknown if supplement needed
- Person info: **NO DOCUMENTATION OF PEOPLE REMOVED**

### Problem 10: No After-Action Debrief
- No structured debrief after execution
- Lessons learned (wrong car lineup, missing charger) not documented
- Same mistakes will happen next time
- No reminder: "Please submit supplements"
- No training identified

---

## SOFTWARE INTERVENTION POINTS

### 1. Digital OPS Plan Form (Pre-Execution)
**Instead of:** Text scattered across messages  
**With Software:** One form, printed/distributed to all 8 people
- Suspect info, photos, address
- Personnel assignments (each person knows their role)
- Equipment list (including phone charger specs)
- Complaint number printed on form
- Timing, briefing location, target photo
- Each person gets a copy before briefing

### 2. Equipment Checklist
**Instead of:** Casual mentions in text  
**With Software:** Supervisor verifies before operation starts
```
Searcher 1: [✓] Vest [✓] Gloves [✓] Flashlight [✓] Radio [✓] Forms
Searcher 2: [✓] Vest [✓] Gloves [✓] Flashlight [✓] Radio [✓] Forms
Searcher 3: [✓] Vest [✓] Gloves [✓] Flashlight [✓] Radio [✓] Forms
Oscar Operator: [✓] Equipment [✓] Battery [✓] Tripod
Sketch Tech: [✓] Digital sketch tool [✓] Tablet
Crime Scene Tech: [✓] Evidence forms [✓] Camera [✓] Chain of custody log
PHONE CHARGER: [✓] USB-C (for target phone)
```
If any unchecked → STOP, get it before execution.

### 3. Role Assignment Cards (Printed for Each Person)
**Instead of:** Verbal briefing (forgotten by execution time)  
**With Software:** Card in each person's hand
```
Your Role: Searcher #1 (Detective Smith)
Responsibility: Search bedroom and closet areas
Report to: Lead Detective Jones
Equipment: Vest, gloves, flashlight, radio, evidence forms
Supplement Required: YES - Searcher report (due 2 days)
Complaint #: 250416-XXXX
```

### 4. Vehicle Staging Map
**Instead of:** "Line up in the neighborhood"  
**With Software:** Live map on everyone's iPad
- Target location (pin)
- Your staging position (with your name)
- Other cars' positions (with names)
- Approach route
- Timing for each car

No scrambling, no wrong lineups.

### 5. Evidence Intake Form (Ready to Go)
**Instead of:** Manual notes on scene  
**With Software:** Digital form with fields
```
Item: Motorola G Power 2022 phone
Location found: Bedroom nightstand
Condition: Battery low (~5%)
Charger found: [ ] Yes [X] No
Photographed by: Crime Scene Tech Jones (11:47am)
Photo ID: #47
Custody transfer: Jones → Brown (11:48am)
[Chain of custody auto-timestamped]
```

### 6. Supplement Assignment & Automated Reminders
**Instead of:** Nobody knows what to file  
**With Software:** Each person knows their supplement, gets reminders
```
Your role: Sketch Tech
Supplement: Digital Crime Scene Sketch
Due: 2 days (April 18)
Tool: Digital sketch app (no paper redraw)
REMINDER SENT: April 17 (1 day before due)
```

### 7. Digital Crime Scene Sketch Tool
**Instead of:** Paper sketch → Manual redraw  
**With Software:** Digital sketching tool
- Sketch on iPad during operation
- Grid overlay for accuracy
- Automatically uploads to case
- No redraw needed
- Digital archive automatically created

### 8. Person Documentation During Search
**Instead of:** No info gathered, lost later  
**With Software:** iPad form during operation
```

Name: ___________
DOB: ___________
ID: ___________
Relationship to suspect: ___________
Contact: ___________

Completed during operation, not after.

### 9. Post-Execution Debrief & Lessons Learned
**Instead of:** No debrief  
**With Software:** Automated prompt
```
What went well?
What went wrong?
What would you do differently?
What equipment was missing?
What training is needed?

[Supervisor compiles responses into report]

**Instead of:** Nobody remembers

- Person info not documented
- No debrief → Same mistakes next time
